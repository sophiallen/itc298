About Us

Transl8 allows anyone with a text-messaging enabled phone to easily and inexpensively access online translation services. Simply text the phrase you want to translate and the desired language to our number, and our app with respond with the translation. Create an online user profile to customize your default settings for even faster and easier translation, see past translations, and create flash cards based on past translations to practice using new words!
